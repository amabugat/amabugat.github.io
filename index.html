About

Hi! My name is Alexis Mabugat

I am currently a student at UC Santa Cruz. I am a third year engineering major, with a concentration in Systems Programming.


While I am experienced in Java, C++, and other languages, I am always eager to learn new languages and softwares. I do a lot of independent projects, mostly catered towards graphic design, website making, or video editing for the clubs I am a part of.


I also have an abundant amount of leadership experience, holding positions in two clubs - Circle K International (part of the Kiwanis Family) and Bayanihan for the past three years. I am currently the president of one of my clubs, Bayanihan. Additionally, I received an award as "Distinguished Secretary" for my role in Circle K International.
